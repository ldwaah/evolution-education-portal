How to Report a Concern

  1. Inform DSL or Deputy DSL immediately.
  2. Record the concern factually on My Concern.
  3. If urgent and no DSL is available:
    • Call MASH: 020 8227 3811
    • Email: childrenss@lbbd.gov.uk
    • Complete a MARF
  4. If a child is in immediate danger: Call 999.
  5. Notify the DSL of any direct referral.
