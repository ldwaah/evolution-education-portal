Best regards, [Pathway Lead Name] Evolution Education [Contact Details]

Level 2

Parent Letter

Letter template to send to parents/carers regarding the Level 2 Behaviour Contract.

Dear [Parent/Carer Name], RE: Level 2 Behaviour Contract – [Student Name] I am writing to inform you that [Student Name] has reached Level 2 of Evolution Education's Behaviour Levels Framework. This means we need to implement a structured Behaviour Contract to support their progress. Why has this contract been issued? [Student Name] has been escalated to Level 2 following [brief reason – e.g., "unsuccessful completion of Level 1" or "persistent behavioural concerns"]. What does this mean? • A formal Behaviour Contract will be put in place for a minimum of 4 weeks • Weekly mentoring and reviews will be provided • Clear SMART targets will be set and monitored • Regular communication with you and the parent school What support will be provided? • Weekly 1:1 mentoring sessions • Weekly review meetings with the Pathway Lead • Access to counselling or Thrive/Boxall support where appropriate • Daily engagement checks and monitoring What we need from you: • Support and reinforcement of expectations at home • Attendance at review meetings when requested • Open communication about any concerns or changes Next steps: We will be arranging a meeting with the parent school to discuss the contract. You will receive a copy of the signed contract once it is finalised, and this will also be uploaded to Bromcom. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact me on [Contact Number] or [Email Address]. Yours sincerely, [Pathway Lead Name] Pathway Lead Evolution Education

Please let us know your earliest availability. Given the seriousness of this stage, it is important that we work together closely to support the student and prevent placement breakdown. Level 3

Parent Letter

Letter template to send to parents/carers regarding the Level 3 Final Warning Contract.

Dear [Parent/Carer Name], RE: Level 3 Final Warning Contract – [Student Name] I am writing to inform you that [Student Name] has reached Level 3 (Final Warning) of Evolution Education's Behaviour Framework. This is the most serious stage and requires immediate attention. Why has this contract been issued? [Student Name] has been escalated to Level 3 following [brief reason – e.g., "unsuccessful completion of Level 2" or "a serious breach of health, safety, or safeguarding expectations"]. This is a FINAL WARNING. This means: • This is the last stage before potential termination of placement • Failure to meet the conditions may result in referral to the CEO and termination of placement • The student must demonstrate immediate and sustained improvement • All expectations are non-negotiable What support will be provided? • Daily check-in/check-out with SLT or Pathway Lead • Enhanced SEMH monitoring and targeted mentoring • Weekly case review with you and the parent school • Risk assessment reviewed and updated weekly • All incidents recorded and reviewed daily What we need from you: • Immediate support and reinforcement of expectations at home • Attendance at all review meetings • Open and honest communication about any concerns • Understanding of the seriousness of this stage Potential outcomes: • Successful completion: contract lifted, return to Level 2 or full reintegration • Extension: further 4-week period with revised support • Referral to CEO: if insufficient improvement, leading to possible termination of placement or temporary remote learning Next steps: We have arranged an urgent meeting with the parent school. You will receive a copy of the signed contract once it is finalised, and this will be uploaded to Bromcom. This is a critical time for [Student Name]. We need to work together to ensure they understand the seriousness of this situation and support them to make the necessary changes. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact me immediately on [Contact Number] or [Email Address]. Yours sincerely, [Pathway Lead Name] Pathway Lead Evolution Education
