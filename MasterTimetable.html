📘 EVOLUTION EDUCATION — COMPLETE STAFF DUTIES (2026 UPDATE)

1️⃣ ALL STAFF — CORE DUTIES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

1. Attendance & Punctuality

  • Arrive on site before contracted time
  • Be in classroom before students arrive
  • Ensure smooth transitions between lessons

2. Behaviour for Learning (BFL)

  • Apply the 3-warning system consistently
  • Record all incidents on Bromcom
  • Use removal, reflection room, and reports in line with policy

3. Safeguarding

  • Record concerns on MyConcern (or designated system)
  • Report immediately to DSL (Lloyd) or CEO in his absence

4. 1:1 Mentoring (Formal Definition)

Every staff member may be required to deliver 1:1 mentoring.

Mentoring MUST:

  • Have a focus (behaviour, wellbeing, academic, reintegration)
  • Be logged on Bromcom the same day
  • Follow Thrive principles (regulate → reflect → repair)
  • Lead to next steps

Not acceptable: informal conversations with no notes

5. Professional Conduct

  • No lateness
  • No unplanned absences
  • Dress code followed
  • Communication professional at all times
