Master Timetable – KS3 & KS4 (Final, All-in-One)

DE'JUANE – KS3 PATHWAY LEAD / HEAD OF SPORT / SEMH AQA

SEMH AQA Delivery (Updated — Clear, Measurable, Accountable)

De'juane now holds FULL responsibility for delivering SEMH AQA units across KS3.

1. Deliver AQA SEMH Sessions

  • 5 sessions per week
  • Structured, planned, and evidenced

2. Links to AQA Must Be CLEAR in Every Session

Every task must include:

  • AQA Unit Title
  • Learning Outcome
  • Evidence Type (worksheet, photo, observation, reflection)

3. Evidence of Student Achievement Required

Evidence may include:

  • Written tasks
  • Photo evidence
  • Practical activity logs
  • Teacher observations
  • Student reflections
  • Completion checklists

4. Completion Expectation (Minimum Standard)

📌 Every two weeks → each student must complete 1 AQA unit.

That means:

  • Minimum 5–6 units per term
  • Units must be achievable and scaffolded

5. Weekly Reporting Requirement

By every Friday 3:00pm, De'juane must send:

  • List of students who completed units
  • Evidence folder (Google Drive or email)
  • Completed AQA unit sheets
  • Students needing additional support

Recipients:

  • Lorelle (AQA verifier)
  • Centre Manager (Lloyd)
  • CEO (Eugene)

He must CC both Centre Manager & CEO every week without fail.
