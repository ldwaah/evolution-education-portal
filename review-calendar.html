Review Calendar — Academic Year 2025–26

This page outlines the review and reporting schedule for all Evolution Education provisions across the 2025–26 academic year.

• Six-weekly reviews are structured progress meetings with students (and schools where applicable). • Key Stage 3 Recovery Placements follow a rolling six-week cycle from their individual start date, excluding half-term breaks, concluding with a 12-week reintegration review.

Key Stage 3 Recovery Placements – Rolling Review Cycle

KS3 Recovery Placements operate on flexible cycles based on each student's start date:

  • Week 6 Review: Mid-point progress evaluation and updated support plan.
  • Week 12 Review: Final review and reintegration planning meeting.

Schools must attend in-person for Level 2 and above; Level 1 reviews may be held remotely.
