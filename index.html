Policy & Procedures

Behaviour for Learning (B4L) Model

Behaviour Warnings

3 Warnings = Reflection (1 day) + 30-minute detention

Parent call/text home to notify of behaviour.

Examples: Being off-task, refusing instructions, disruptive behaviour, getting out of chair without permission, throwing objects, talking back, inappropriate uniform.

Tier 2

Reflection (2 days) + 30-minute detention per day

Student placed on report or behaviour contract to Head of Key Stage. Parental meeting arranged.

Examples: Swearing in class, verbal abuse towards student, damage to property/misuse of equipment.

Tier 3

Incident referred to CEO. Parent school notified.

Examples: Abuse (physical/verbal) towards staff, physical abuse towards student, vaping, cyber-bullying/bullying, stealing/vandalism, persistent Tier 2 behaviour or failed behaviour contract.

Reflection Room

A place for students to reflect on behaviour that resulted in removal from lessons.

Students complete:

  • Behaviour booklet supporting reflection on specific concerns
  • Classroom work set by teachers

Rules:

  • No speaking to other students
  • Complete all work set

Note: Failing to follow rules in reflection room will result in escalation to Tier 2.
