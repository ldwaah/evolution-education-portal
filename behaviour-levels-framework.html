EVOLUTION EDUCATION

BEHAVIOUR LEVELS FRAMEWORK & STAFF HANDBOOK
(2025/26)
1. PURPOSE

This handbook outlines Evolution Education's behaviour management system, in line with:

  • DfE Alternative Provision Guidance 2025
  • Ofsted AP Framework 2025
  • LBBD Off-Site Direction Regulations
  • Governance of Mayesbrook Park School (URN holder for our provision)
  • LBBD Quality Assurance responsibilities

This handbook ensures every staff member follows a unified, consistent, compliant approach to behaviour management, escalation, documentation, and communication.

2. BEHAVIOUR LEVELS OVERVIEW

Evolution Education uses a clear 3-stage escalation pathway:

LEVEL 1 – REPORT STAGE
Minimum Duration: 2 weeks
Lead: Pathway Lead
Purpose: Early intervention and monitoring
Support: Daily report, weekly review, parent-school updates
LEVEL 2 – BEHAVIOUR CONTRACT
Minimum Duration: 4 weeks
Lead: Pathway Lead + CEO
Purpose: Structured intervention with SMART targets
Support: Weekly mentoring, counselling if required
LEVEL 3 – FINAL WARNING CONTRACT
Minimum Duration: 2 weeks
Lead: CEO
Purpose: Final chance before referral review
⚠️ Important Notes:
  • Students may skip stages if behaviour presents a Health & Safety risk.
  • All behaviour must be logged on Bromcom.
